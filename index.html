My Story

What I lack in career accolades and higher education, I make up for in rich life experiences. My path has been nontradtional to say the least, with my 20's being a whirlwind of creative ambition and dead end jobs, dotted with occasional troublemaking. My background is in music and video, having made my first video at 8 years old and my first song at 17. Though I still enjoy the arts (and can still kick a sick freestyle on command), part of suceeding is knowing when to quit. I pursued my goals to a logical conclusion and went on a cross-country and international sabbatical.

Holiday Road and Viva Mexico

In May of 2020 I left Los Angeles and began volunteering on farms across the US using a platform called Workaway. It pairs penny pinching and adventurous travelers with property and land owners who are looking for help on the cheap. Eventually, I decided to travel to Mexico and take the same approach, though with less of a focus on rural farms, instead opting to work at various hostels. Increasing lockdown restrictions saw me stuck in Mexico, and I ended up living there for nearly 2 years. Last October, my Dad ran into some health issues, so I moved to Texas to help him tend to his land. Thanks to my parents generosity, I have considerable free time to alter my life trajectory by learning to code.

Why Should You Give Me A Chance?
